Ruby Terminal App Assessment

Our first assessment was a Ruby Terminal App Assessment. I created together with my classmate Jamie Mills. Above is a photo of us presenting our app to the class.

Our app is a shopping list where you can add items and quantities. You can select a category for each of your items. You have an option to view your current shopping list as well. When you're done, you can view your final shopping list and everything's sorted by category.

Looking back I am amazed that we managed to designed and built our app in only two days. It was an amazing experience and I learnt so much. My favourite part besides coding was doing pair-programming.
