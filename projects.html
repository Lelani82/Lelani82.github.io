PROJECTS

Ruby Assessment

Our first assessment was a Ruby Terminal App. I created it together with my classmate Jamie. Here's a photo of us presenting our app to the class:

Our app is a shopping list where you can add items and quantities. You can select a category for each of your items. You have an option to view your current shopping list as well. When you're done, you can view your final shopping list and everything's sorted by category.

Looking back I am amazed that we managed to designed and build our app in only two days. It was a wonderful experience and I learnt so much. My favourite part besides coding was doing pair-programming.

If you would like to check it out, take a look at our Git Repo.

Ruby on Rails Assessment

Our third assessment was a Two-Sided Marketplace App using Ruby on Rails. I created it together with Rachel, another one of my classmates. Here's a screenshot of our Home page:

Our app allows a user to buy or sell high-end used shoes. Shoes are categorised by Boots, Flats, Heels and Trainers.

To date, this is the second-largest project that I have worked on. I enjoyed the project management and wireframe creation. My favourite part was using the Agile Methodology.

JavaScript API Hackathon

After learning Vanilla JavaScript we had a class hackathon. We had an opportunity to create a website using API's. Together with two of my classmates, we created the Tony Time Website.

It is a cocktail selection website where you can search for cocktails by name or ingredient. The name is in honour one of our amazing friends, Tony. He is such an incredible, kindhearted individual and we always have a fun, happy time when he's around.

We spent two days on the hackathon creating the website. Our team also ended up winning the hackathon which was a wonderful experience!
