My Coding Journey

Back in 2018, I got my first taste of coding. It was a beginner's course in HTML & CSS at the University of the Sunshine Coast. Even before our first lesson ended, I knew that I had found my passion. Little did I know, but this was the start of my incredible coding journey ... and my career change!

Soon afterwards I went on to do a WordPress website creation course, as well as my first workshop - Rails Girls 2018. My first coding workshop was an amazing experience. Even though I was clueless about what was going on, I am so thankful to all the wonderful volunteers. It was incredible to see how a community offered their time to come together to help support others. I knew that I wanted to be a part of this industry and that one day too, I could volunteer myself.

After the Rails Girls workshop, I attended my next workshops - NodeGirls 2018 and Muses 2019. Both were such incredible experiences, I decided I wanted a career change so I chose to study further.

I ended up choosing to study through Coder Academy's GenTech Bootcamp. It is a 10-month, part-time course which I completed in January 2020. Attending a Bootcamp was challenging at times, but I loved every minute of it. I enjoyed being part of a supportive team environment and met some amazing people!

During our course, we learnt the Command Line, Git, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, HTML, CSS, JavaScript and the MERN stack. We also learned project management skills. The course has a very hands-on approach, which made it a wonderful learning experience.

For our assessments, we completed a Portfolio Website, a Ruby Terminal App, a Two-Sided Marketplace using Ruby on Rails and a MERN app for a client (please see my Projects page).

After completing my course I went on to intern at Queensland Urban Utilities. It was such a fantastic experience being able to contribute to real projects. I got to learn so much each day and I was so lucky to be part of their amazing team culture!

Fast-forward to today ... here I am, so grateful that I went on this journey and that I get to pursue my passion every day!
